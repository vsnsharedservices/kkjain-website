Krishan Kumar Jain & Co. is a full-service accounting, auditing, and business process offshoring firm inter-alia practicing in the area of Accounting, Audit & Assurance, Book Keeping, Tax Advisory, and Compliances, Payroll Processing, Fund Management, VCFO or part-time CFO services, Budgeting and Compliance Support.

We understand the complexities of both offshore and onshore business processes. Accordingly, our team provides solutions to clients within the best practices and per International Standards.

We established our practice in 1995 with a commitment to providing the best and most comprehensive services to all our clients. We are acclaimed for our highly efficient, proficient, and experienced team of professionals, having a personalized approach while deliberating over the issues.

There is no magic wand that can resolve our problems. The solution rests with our work and discipline.
